FAQ

Clear answers before you publish, install, or build.

ProxyNova is intentionally local and open source. These answers explain what the app does, what it stores, and how to use it responsibly.

What is ProxyNova?

ProxyNova is a Windows desktop app for checking, scraping, grading, filtering, and exporting proxy lists. It is built with Go, Wails, React, and TypeScript.

Does ProxyNova send my proxy lists to a server?

No. ProxyNova is a local desktop tool. Proxy lists, settings, source configuration, judge URLs, and exports stay on the machine running the app unless you manually share them.

Which protocols does it check?

ProxyNova supports HTTP, HTTPS, SOCKS4, and SOCKS5 checks. Results can be filtered by protocol after validation.

What do Elite, Anonymous, and Transparent mean?

Those labels describe what the judge endpoint sees. Elite proxies avoid obvious proxy headers and origin leaks. Anonymous proxies hide the origin but may reveal proxy usage. Transparent proxies work but expose identifying headers or the client origin.

Why do many free proxies fail?

Public proxies are often overloaded, rate-limited, misconfigured, region-blocked, or already dead by the time a list is scraped. Timeout, retry, and source-quality settings matter more than raw list size.

Do I need GeoLite2?

GeoLite2-City.mmdb is required for offline country and city lookup in source builds. Without it, geography features cannot be embedded into the binary.

What formats can I export?

ProxyNova exports filtered results as TXT, CSV, and JSON. TXT is best for simple IP:PORT lists, CSV is best for spreadsheet review, and JSON is best for automation.

Is macOS or Linux supported?

The published build target is Windows. Because the project uses Go and Wails, other platforms may be possible from source, but they are not presented here as official published builds.

Can I use ProxyNova for automation against third-party services?

Only use proxies and targets you are authorized to test. ProxyNova should not be used for spam, credential attacks, scraping against terms of service, access-control evasion, or traffic abuse.

Where do I report issues?

Use the GitHub repository for bugs, feature requests, and source review: github.com/savxzthc/ProxyNova.
